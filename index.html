How scoring works

Place a piece+1 point per block A 4-block L piece pays +4
Clear a setfill a whole row, column, or 3x3 box: +18 Finish a row with a 2-block piece: 2 + 18 = +20
Combosets cleared together are worth much more: x2 is +54, x3 is +90, x4 is +126... One block lands the last cell of a row AND a column at once: +54 instead of 18 + 18
Icon match3 or more matching icons inside a cleared set: 3-4 icons +10, 5-6 +25, 7-8 +50 A cleared row holding 5 stars adds +25
Perfect matchall 9 icons in a set the same: +100 A 3x3 box of 9 strawberries adds +100
Matching setsthe same icon matched across 2, 3, or 4+ sets cleared together: +50 / +100 / +200 A row and a column clear together, both with 3+ flowers: +50 on top of both flower bonuses
Lizard power🦎 bonuses always count double 3 lizards pay +20, and a perfect lizard set pays +200
🔥 Streakclear on back-to-back placements: x2 +10, x3 +20, x4 +30... A cold placement cools it; a frozen set keeps it warm until the melt

Earning items

🔄 Rotateone for every 200 points you score One Rotate spins that piece as many times as you like until you place it. Spin it back to the start and the Rotate returns to your pocket
↩️ Undoone for every 2nd combo (x2 or bigger) Two x2 combos in one game = one Undo; it even works on the game-over screen
❄️ Freezeone per Perfect Match, every 2nd combo, and each x3 streak Ice a piece, freeze a row, stack more, then melt it all into one clear for a combo
🎲 Rerollone per x3+ combo, each x4 streak, and every Matching Sets combo Tap 🎲, then tap a tray piece to swap it for a brand new one
You can hold at most 3 of each; extras are lost, so spend them!
